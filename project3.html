Usability Tests

About the Usability Tests

For this project, I went through the entire process of administering a usability test. I started by writing out a script; I thought out some questions that would help me gauge how effect my guides were and highlight any improvements I needed to make. After my script was written, I wrote out consent forms for my particpants. Once I had signed consent forms for my participants, I completed usability tests for both my Print User Guide and my Video User Guide following the scripts I'd prepared.

Skills Gained

This was one of my favorite projects in this course, and I feel like I gained a lot from it. For one, I got practice writing professionally in both my script and my consent forms. What I really enjoyed, though, was the actual usability testing. Throughout my tests, I practiced professionally hosting a meeting, communicating, and active listening. I also got practice in note-taking during a meeting, which I will definitely use in my future career.
