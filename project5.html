User Story

About the Project

The User Story project involved creating profiles for the participants in my Usability Tests. For this project, I considered the demographics and psychographics of my participants.

Skills Gained

In working on my User Story, I gained research skills that will help me in many of my future endeavors, both as a student and a working professional. This project helped to hone my ability to detect patterns and commonalities among users to create realistic profiles. I also gained skill in user-centered thinking, which will definitely serve useful in my future career.
