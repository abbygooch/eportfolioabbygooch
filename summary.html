Creating User Documentation: What I Gained

I also practiced with creating user profiles and understanding differernt users and their diverse needs. Overall, this class gave me much needed perspective on a relevant topic. As an Advertising major planning to work in the communications field, I expect to use the skills I gained in this course a lot throughout my career.
