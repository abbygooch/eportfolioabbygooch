About the Usability Test Report

The Usability Test Report was essentially a reflection on my Usability Tests. This report detailed how I conducted my usability tests, how they went, and the conclusions I had drawn based on them.

Skills Gained

I consider any report writing valuable practice; as a communications major, professional writing is very important to my success. Moreover, this report gave me practice in considering accessibility and ease of something I produced, which isn't something I'd considered much in the past. I gained critical thinking skills in my reflection of the test results, and I improved on my attention to detail.
