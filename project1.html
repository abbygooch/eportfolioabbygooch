Print User Guide

About the Print User Guide

For this project, I wrote out a step by step guide for how to choose a template on Canva. I went through the process of choosing a template once myself, and then once more to gather screenshots I thought would help users follow my guide.

Skills Gained

While working on this project, I enhanced my technical writing skills, particularly in structuring content for diverse audiences and ensuring accessibility for all users. I made sure each step in my guide was written in plain language and ensured my screenshots were clear and informative, both of which are important skills to have in any future career, especially one in the communications field.
